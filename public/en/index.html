OPF event: tools for accessible and inclusive publishing worflows

During Open Publishing Fest, a decentralized public event that brings together communities supporting open source software, open content, and open publishing models, editadapat presents some tools to ensure our worklows delivers contents accessibles to a larger readers needs.

Digital publication is a technical oportunity to improve access to knowledge for people with print disabilities. A journey into cultural exchange beetwen accessibility specialized organizations and publishers that leads us into some tools and practices for all readers benefit.
